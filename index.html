Welcome to the world of PORTALS! In this interactive story, you will make choices that will determine the outcome of your adventure. Choose wisely!

You wake up in a silent, ancient library that only appears to those on the brink of a life. Every book on the shelves contains a different version of your life and what couldve been. The pasts you never lived, futures that haven't had the chances to happen yet, and choices youve yet come across to make. As you walk further you see three books appear in front of you.
