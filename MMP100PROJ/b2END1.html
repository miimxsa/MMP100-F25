You walk the endless hallway, expecting more silence but a soft meow echoes ahead. You follow it. The air warms. The walls shift, opening into a sunlit garden hidden between realities. Cats literally could be thousands of them lounging, playing, napping in patches of light. They look up as you enter. One with a little crown pads over and nods . You are now one of them. You curl up in the sun. The hallway is gone.
